SECTION 1

Welcome to SlotIQ

SlotIQ turns every slot session you play into real intelligence about how you play. Log your visits and the app quietly builds a clear picture of your performance, your habits, and your edge — all from your own real data. No guesswork, no gimmicks.

This guide walks a brand-new player through the whole app: a quick first-session tutorial, then a detailed tour of every screen. By the end you'll know exactly what every number means and how to use it.

The one idea behind everything

SlotIQ is built on a simple promise: patterns, never predictions. It will never tell you a machine is "due" or which game to play next — no app can know that. Instead, it reflects what has actually happened in your history (and, anonymously, the community's), so you can make calmer, better-informed decisions.

Free to use, forever Tracking your sessions and seeing your real totals is always free. The deeper intelligence — Player IQ, Gambling DNA, Forecast, and Community — is a premium upgrade ($12.99/mo or $89.99/yr), shown blurred in the app so you always know what's there before you unlock it.

SECTION 3

The Dashboard & your Player IQ

Your home screen is your command center. The hero card is the Player IQ Score — a single 0–100 number that captures your skill as a player and climbs as you sharpen up.

What the Player IQ Score means

It's a weighted blend of your healthiest habits: discipline, cash-out timing, consistency, staying calm after losses, protecting your profit, and steady bet sizing.

The four headline stats

Sessions is how many visits you've logged. Win Rate is the share you finished ahead. Avg RTP is money out ÷ money in (over 100% means you're up overall). Net Result is your lifetime profit or loss.

Your Player IQ tiers

Beginner0–49Just getting started — keep logging.
Intermediate50–69"Developing Player" — solid habits forming.
Advanced70–89Disciplined and consistent.
Elite90–100Top-tier control and protection.
Your Numbers — always free Below the IQ card, the Your Numbers panel shows your real totals: Lifetime Net, Sessions, Time Played, Biggest Win, Biggest Loss, and your win–loss Record. Free for everyone, forever.
SECTION 4

Sessions: track every visit

The Sessions tab is where it all begins — every visit you log feeds everything else.

Starting and ending a session

Tap Start Session, pick your casino (it autofills), add the machine and your money in, and you're tracking. When you're done, End Session asks for your cash-out and a couple of details, then saves it.

Simple vs. Advanced mode

Simple is fast: money in, money out, done. Advanced tracks denomination and bet changes live as you play, which makes your Gambling DNA richer and more accurate.

Reading your history

The Profit / Loss over time chart turns your sessions into a running line of your bankroll — green when you're up, red when you're down. Below it, Session History lists every visit with the date, machine, bet, denomination, casino and your in/out. Tap a session for a per-machine breakdown; tap a machine for its full Machine Analysis.

Use the Machine ID field When you add a machine there's a spot for the house Machine ID (the number on the cabinet). It's optional, but filling it in lets SlotIQ tell two "Dragon Link" cabinets apart and gives you precise, per-cabinet analysis.
SECTION 5

Gambling DNA: how you really play

Your Gambling DNA is a one-of-a-kind profile of how you play — not just whether you won. The radar scores you on eight traits and overlays the average of all SlotIQ players.

Your player type

At the top, SlotIQ names your player type — a plain-English summary of your style, like "Chaser" or "Sniper." It's the headline of your DNA; the eight traits below explain why.

The eight traits, decoded

DisciplineHigh: you stick to a plan and stop near your goals.Low: sessions run long and loose.
Cash-Out DisciplineHigh: you leave with your money.Low: you give wins back before cashing out.
Chasing ControlHigh: you stay calm after a loss.Low: you reload or bet up to recover.
Bet ControlHigh: steady, consistent stake sizing.Low: bets jump around a lot.
Session ConsistencyHigh: repeatable, steady results.Low: big swings session to session.
Emotional StabilityHigh: even-keeled across visits.Low: reactive, mood-driven play.
Machine LoyaltyHigh: you focus on a core set of machines.Low: you hop around constantly.
Profit ProtectionHigh: your wins are big and well-kept.Low: wins are thin or given back.

What it means to be each player type

Your type is the single pattern that defines your play right now. None is "bad" — each has a real strength and one thing worth watching.

The Chaser

You extend sessions and reload after losses; you play with heart and persistence. Watch: losses can snowball — set a stop-loss and keep your stake steady after a downswing.

The Grinder

Consistent, steady, low-drama play that books small wins reliably. Watch: you can plateau — your discipline can support the occasional calculated bigger shot.

The Sniper

Focused on a small set of machines you know well, with sharp cash-out timing. Watch: tunnel vision — glance at Community to confirm your machines are still the strong ones.

The High Roller

Big average buy-ins and the nerve to match. Watch: variance is brutal at high stakes — lean on Cash-Out Discipline to bank profit.

The Explorer

You sample lots of different machines and love variety. Watch: low machine loyalty makes your patterns noisy — settle in to truly learn a few cabinets.

The Disciplined Player

Textbook control: you set goals, leave with money, and keep stakes even. Watch: keep it up — this is the profile that climbs to Elite.

The Opportunist

Adaptable, with no single dominant pattern yet. Watch: build consistency — a steadier rhythm is the fastest way to raise your Player IQ.

How your type is chosen SlotIQ reads your real sessions — how long you play, how you bet after losses, how many machines you touch, how much of your peak you keep — and matches the strongest signal to a type. Play differently over time and your type changes with you.
SECTION 6

The Forecast Center: your edge, uncovered

The Forecast Center surfaces where your tracked sessions have run strongest — your best machines, days and setups — drawn entirely from your own history.

Your best-fit profile

The top card shows the machine, denomination, bet range and session length where you've performed best, along with your win rate there.

Best days and peak window

A bar chart highlights your strongest day historically, and your peak window points to the time of day your sessions have gone best.

Patterns from your past — not a crystal ball Every insight here is historical and descriptive. "Wednesday has been your strongest day" means exactly that — what happened, not a promise about next Wednesday. Use it to understand your tendencies, never as a prediction of outcomes.
SECTION 7

Community insights: the power of pooled data

The Community board pools anonymized data from every SlotIQ player into one place: real RTP across machines, casinos and states.

Reading the board

You'll see the highest and lowest community RTP by machine (grouped by name and version), plus how many sessions the community has pooled. Filter by your state and casino.

Tap a machine for the deep dive

Tapping any machine opens a full insights page: community RTP, players and plays, hit rate, biggest logged win, an RTP-over-time trend, plus breakdowns by casino, time of day and denomination — and how it ranks.

Anonymous, honest, and historical Community data is aggregated and anonymized — counts and averages, never anyone's identity. A machine only appears once enough different players have logged it, so it can never be one person's data in disguise. And like everything in SlotIQ, it's a historical record — not a "what to play" recommendation.
