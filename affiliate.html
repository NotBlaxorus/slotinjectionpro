Questions, answered.

How do commissions work?
You earn a percentage of each new subscriber's first payment (monthly or annual plan) when they sign up using your code. One commission per subscriber.

Where is my dashboard?
Inside the SlotIQ app: open the menu and tap Affiliate Dashboard. It shows earnings, referrals, payout history, and your payment settings.

How do payouts work?
Monthly, via PayPal or bank transfer — set your details in the app. Every payout is recorded in your dashboard.

Is there a minimum audience size?
No. If you can share responsibly and add value for your audience, you can apply.

What does my audience get?
An extended free trial of SlotIQ Premium when they use your code — a real perk, not just a tracking link.

Can I promote anywhere?
Yes — social, video, email, private groups, and communities. Please keep it honest and avoid spam.
