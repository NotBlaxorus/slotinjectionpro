Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/07/2025

Slot Injection Pro (“App”), operated by Slot Injection LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the App. By accessing or using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.


For users in the European Union (EU), this Privacy Policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For users in California, this Privacy Policy complies with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).


1. Information We Collect


1.1. Account Information:
We collect information you provide during registration, including your name, email address, and username.


1.2. User-Generated Data:
The App collects session data, such as slot machine details, Return to Player (RTP) percentages, and session statistics. This data is linked to your account for personalized insights but is anonymized and aggregated when included in the shared data pool.


1.3. Usage Data:
We collect data about your interactions with the App, including session duration, feature usage, and group activity.


1.4. Device Information:
We collect non-personal information about the device you use to access the App, such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.


1.5. Payment Information:
All payments for subscriptions are processed securely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. Payments are governed by the respective privacy policies of these platforms:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Your Information


2.1. To Provide Services:
Your data is used to deliver App functionality, such as tracking gameplay sessions, generating insights, and enabling group features.


2.2. Shared Data Pool:
Anonymized session data may contribute to the shared data pool visible to other App users. This data is aggregated and does not include any account identifiers, such as your name, email, or user ID.


2.3. Personal Insights:
Your data is used to create personalized reports and statistics for your account.


2.4. App Improvements:
We use usage data to identify trends, improve features, and ensure App security and performance.


2.5. Compliance and Legal Obligations:
We may use your information to comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

3. Your Rights (GDPR and CCPA)


3.1. Rights Under GDPR:
If you are located in the EU, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal data (“Right to Be Forgotten”).
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how your data is processed.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.


3.2. Rights Under CCPA:
If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request to know what personal data we collect, use, and share.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale of personal data (we do not sell personal data).
  • Non-Discrimination: You have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise your GDPR or CCPA rights, please contact us at: support@slotinjectionpro.com .

4. How We Protect Your Information


4.1. Data Security:
We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.


4.2. Encryption:
Sensitive information, such as login credentials, is encrypted during transmission and storage.


4.3. Access Controls:
Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Sharing of Information


5.1. Aggregated and Anonymized Data:
Aggregated session data is shared in the App’s shared data pool. This data is anonymized and does not identify individual users or accounts.


5.2. Third-Party Payment Processors:
All payments for subscriptions are processed securely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. These platforms handle your payment information in accordance with their privacy policies.


5.3. Legal Requirements:
We may disclose your data to comply with legal obligations or protect the rights and safety of our users and others.

6. Data Retention


6.1. Personal Data:
We retain personal data for as long as your account is active or as required to provide services.


6.2. Anonymized Data:
Aggregated and anonymized session data may be retained indefinitely for statistical and analytical purposes.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy


We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. Any changes will be effective when posted. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
